Soda Springs Lava Formations

The main attraction in Soda Springs, Idaho is the world’s only “captive“ geyser. Created in 1937 by an individual seeking a source of heated water to build a swimming pool, this geyser has no naturally self-contained limits. It would shoot forth continuously if not capped and it’s performance limited by man.

Not only is there a boardwalk around the geyser and benches on which to sit and watch, people have been known to take their shoes off and walk onto the geyser surface, thus enjoying the spray and runoff from the geyser as it erupts every hour.

Interesting lava formations created by past volcanic activity dot the landscape and make for fun adventures.
